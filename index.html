Introduction

I'm Hao Yi, a Ph.D. candidate of Xidian University , supervised by Prof. Gao Xinbo. I'm conducting my research at HIT, VIPLab, State Key Laboratory of ISN. My research interests in deep learning, computer vision pattern recognition with applications to video surveillance, especially on person re-identification and video anomaly detection.
